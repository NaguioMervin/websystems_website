Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) Program Objectives

In pursuit of its goals, the Department shall endeavor to achieve the following specific objectives:

  • To provide students to endure with a life-long learning and improve performance as a foundation for continuing professional development.
  • To involve and encourage students to produce researchers relevant with institutional, regional, national and globally priorities that will be able to address the needs of the community they serve.
  • To provide a new technologies or programs that is being used globally.
  • To provide avenue for graduates employability through IT industry linkages.


Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) Program Objectives

The BSCS program will equip learners with:

  • Computer science theories and methodologies and new technologies in the design and development of computing solutions with appropriate consideration to public health and safety, culture, society, gender and environment.
  • Personal development and lifelong learning attitudes through research and advance studies, and recognize the professional, legal, security, and ethical issues relevant to computing career practice.
  • Communication and collaborative skills in diverse teams and multidisciplinary settings.
